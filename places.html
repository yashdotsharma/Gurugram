Fun Places

Kingdom of Dreams

The Kingdom of Dreams is one of the best places to visit in Gurgaon. It is India’s first live entertainment zone, offering a leisure destination and a theater, that creates an unforgettable experience like you have never seen. It brings you to a different level of the rich cultural heritage of India, with its many events and performances. The Kingdom of Dreams covers almost 6 acres (2.4 hectares) and it is associated with many Bollywood stars, making it known as the place where “Bollywood meets Broadway” and it aims to keep the culture of India alive, through drama and music.

Address: Auditorium Complex, Near IFFCO Metro Station, Sector 29, Gurugram Website: Kingdom of Dreams

Eat parathas in Murthal

Do you love parathas? Parathas are a type of unleavened Indian flatbread, often stuffed with delicious fillings. If you want to try this delicious food, don’t miss a visit to Murthal. Murthal is located on National Highway 1, connecting Delhi to Amritsar. Known as the “Paratha Capital of India”, this town offers the most mouthwatering parathas. It was originally established as a quick stop for truck drivers to grab a bite and over time, it gained popularity.

Address: Murthal Dhaba, Kotwan, Haryana

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary

Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary is a very famous weekend place and is named as a National Park by the Haryana Government. This bird sanctuary is ideal for bird watchers and the best time to visit is during the winter, when a large number of migratory birds, stop here.

Sheetla Mata Mandir

Sheetla Mata Temple is an ancient temple dedicated to the Mata Sheetla Devi, wife of Guru Dronacharya who was the teacher of the Pandavas and Kauravas according to an Indian epic Mahabharata. The temple is located on Sheetla Mata Road in Gurugram city of Gurugram district in the state of Haryana in India. Wikipedia Address: Sheetla Mata Rd, Masani Village, Sector 6, Gurugram, Haryana 122001.
