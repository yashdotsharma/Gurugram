Shopping

MGF Mall

The MGF Mall is located just next to the MG Road metro station and so it is easily accessible. It has a good variety of fashion brands, along with some fine, delicious food. If you just want to relax and don’t feel like walking around, you can check out a movie at this mall. You can also definitely find some good clothes, here.

Address: MG Road, Heritage City, Sector 25, Gurugram, Haryana 122002

Ambience Mall

Generally, most larger buildings in Gurgaon are offices or apartment buildings. But nestled among them, there is a heaven for those who love to stop. The Ambience Mall must be at the top of every shoper’s list. Have you heard about Galleria? This is the place if you are looking for some high end, expensive products. And if you aren’t such a brand freak, you can check out the other places, like Qutub Plaza, Sadar Bazaar and Arjun Marg.

Address: Ambience Island, NH- 8, DLF-3, Sector-24, Gurugram, Haryana 122002 Website: Ambience Mall
