Night Life

Vapour Bar Exchange

Vapour Bar Exchange is located in Sector 48, Gurgaon, which is one of the top hubs in town. Set up in nice brown and green tone, Vapour Bar Exchange is a nightclub which offers delicious Finger Food, Continental, Chinese & North Indian cuisines

Manhattan Brewery & Bar Exchange

Another one in the same block and with an equally stunning ambiance, and music Manhattan Brewery has a lot to offer. It's famous for having virtual games and the Xbox! What's more, they're a microbrewery as well! If you're crazy about partying, then Manhattan should definitely be on your list, especially on Saturday.
